About

Play classic arcade games for FREE!

Our Mission

Our mission is to bring the joy of classic arcade games to a new generation of players. We believe that everyone should have access to fun and engaging games, regardless of their age or experience level. That's why we've created The Arcade - a place where you can play all your favourite games for free, without any ads or in-app purchases. We hope you enjoy playing our games as much as we enjoyed making them!
